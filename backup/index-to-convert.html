Zoe's Story

Zoe Conway is your average 24 year old loving life and living it to the max when in October last year, after suffering severe headaches for two weeks, she went to her doctors and was referred immediately to Frimley park Hospital to have her eyes checked as some bleeding was found behind her eyes. From that she was admitted into hospital and bloods taken. A consultant was called in and from there she was rushed by Ambulance to St Georges Hospital and admitted to Ruth Myles. The shock and disbelief cannot be described and this Young Girl has fought every day to get better, the care and support given by superb Staff at St Georges cannot be praised enough and with their care we are hopeful for a positive future.
